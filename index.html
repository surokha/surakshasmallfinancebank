About Suraksha Small Finance Bank

Suraksha Small Finance Bank is a finance company and is recognised under section 406 of the Companies Act, 2013(Earlier 620A RBI Act.). It is regulated and registered by Ministry Of Corporate Affairs, Government Of India and directed by Reserve Bank Of India in matters relating to it's deposite acceptance activities. It lends and borrows money among it's members. It provides financial services to the unserved and underserved members and transforms to a mass market bank.
